Release notes

What’s new and what’s fixed in recent versions.

1.2.0

  • Backup & restore — export all your data (invoices, clients, settings, PDFs, logo, signature) to a ZIP file from My details, and restore it on a new device.
  • Send invoices from client card — select one or more invoices on a client's detail screen and share them via the share sheet or email.
  • Share PDF / Send by email — when sharing an invoice you now choose between the share sheet (WhatsApp, SMS …) or a pre-filled email with subject and body.
  • Invoice preview from client — tap an invoice in the client's invoice list to open and edit it.
  • Client autocomplete on invoice import — when importing a PDF invoice, pick a client from the list or type a new name that is automatically saved.
  • Fixed grouping of invoices under Unknown client — all variants (with or without name) are now correctly combined.
  • Fixed restoring the Name/Surname field when editing an Unknown client invoice.

1.1.0

  • Year-by-year overview — the Invoices tab now shows statistics for each year: total invoices, paid, open, and totals.
  • Invoice filter — quickly filter between all, paid, and open invoices.
  • CSV export — export this year's invoices or a manual selection to an Excel-compatible CSV file.
  • Total in selection mode — when selecting invoices, the app bar immediately shows the combined amount.
  • More readable service date on PDF — date now shown as e.g. "15 January 2025" instead of "15.01.2025".
  • Customer form improvements and duplicate prevention.
  • Mini analytics on customer card (invoices count, paid, open, amounts).

1.0.x

  • Improvements to PDF invoices (UPN / VAT recap layout).
  • Fixes for PDF file paths after app upgrades.
  • UX improvements for multi-item invoice entry.
